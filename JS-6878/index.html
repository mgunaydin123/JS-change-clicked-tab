London

London is the capital of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 9 million inhabitants.

Paris

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France, with an estimated population of 2,175,601 residents as of 2018, in an area of more than 105 square kilometres (41 square miles).

Tokyo

Tokyo, officially Tokyo Metropolis, is the capital of Japan and one of its 47 prefectures. The metropolitan area of Tokyo is the most populous metropolitan area in the world.
